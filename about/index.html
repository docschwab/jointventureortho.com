About Joint Venture Orthopaedics

Bringing evidence-based orthopaedic research to surgeons worldwide through AI-powered media.

Our Mission

The orthopaedic literature moves fast. Hundreds of open-access studies are published every week across dozens of journals and seven major subspecialties. No surgeon has time to read them all.

Joint Venture Orthopaedics uses artificial intelligence to curate, summarize, and deliver the research that matters to your practice. Our goal is simple: a surgeon anywhere in the world should be able to stay current on the literature in under 15 minutes a day, in their own language, for free.

What We Do

Every piece of content we produce starts with the primary literature. We search PubMed for the latest open-access manuscripts, retrieve full text, and use AI to generate clinically actionable summaries. Every manuscript is cited with a DOI link so you can read the original source. Every summary includes a "So what can you do differently?" section grounded in the evidence.

We believe the best orthopaedic education is evidence-based, accessible, and practical. That is what Joint Venture Orthopaedics delivers.
